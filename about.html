About Me

Hi, I'm Edwin Kimuruae

I am a passionate Web Developer with experience in creating dynamic and user-friendly websites. My journey in web development started with a curiosity about how websites work, and over the years, it has turned into a full-fledged career that I love.

With a background in computer science and a deep understanding of modern web technologies, I specialize in both front-end and back-end development. My goal is to build web solutions that not only meet the needs of clients but also provide an exceptional user experience.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy learning about new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and sharing knowledge with the developer community. In my free time, I also like to explore the outdoors, read books, and spend time with family and friends.
